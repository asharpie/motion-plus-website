The Problem

Winter accessibility is a blind spot in the wheelchair market. 95% of wheelchair users report getting stuck in snow, and 42% reduce their outdoor activities during winter months.

Existing solutions—tire chains, manual wrapping, asking for help—are slow, complicated, and unreliable. Wheelchair users deserve freedom and independence year-round, not seasonal limitations.

The Solution

Wrapid is a magnetic all-terrain wheel cover designed specifically for wheelchairs. It installs in under 60 seconds with no tools or assistance, grips any terrain, and removes just as fast.

We've validated the concept with UA Adapted Athletics and earned 2nd Place at the UA River Pitch Competition and 2nd Place at UA Big Ideas. With a production cost of ~$35 at scale and a target price of $79–$99, we project 65% gross margin and ~150 units sold in Year 1.
