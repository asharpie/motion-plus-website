Ordering FAQ

How does ordering work?

Submit your order request with product details and shipping info. We'll email you within 1-2 business days to confirm your order and arrange a payment method (Venmo, PayPal, or bank transfer).

How long does shipping take?

Custom products (Shin Sheath, Wrapid) are made-to-order and typically ship within 2-3 weeks. Standard products (U-Clamp) ship within 5-7 business days.

What if I don't know my measurements?

No worries! Submit your order request and note that you need measurement guidance. We'll send you a detailed measurement guide with photos and instructions.
