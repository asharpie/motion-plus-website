How It Works

Planetary Gear System

The Mech Chair uses an innovative planetary gear wheel configuration that multiplies your propulsion force without any external power source. This mechanical advantage allows users to travel faster or push with less effort—choose your mode based on terrain and energy level.

The system is durable, maintenance-friendly, and integrates seamlessly with standard wheelchairs. It's designed for long-term reliability in daily use.

Market Impact

Addressing Global Need

Approximately 15 million people worldwide live with spinal cord injuries. Many, particularly those with C-4 and C-5 level injuries, face a difficult choice: exhaust themselves with standard manual wheelchairs or spend thousands on powered alternatives.

The Mech Chair fills this gap with an affordable, reliable solution that delivers real mechanical advantage without the cost or dependency of powered systems.
