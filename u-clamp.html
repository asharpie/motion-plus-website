The Problem

Specialized wheelchair scooters are heavy, slow, and expensive—often costing $2,500 or more. They're also permanent attachments that leave hardware bolted to your chair, limiting flexibility and mobility options.

The Solution

U-Clamp lets you use any standard electric scooter with your wheelchair. Attach in seconds, remove in seconds. At $200 (with a $200 scooter = $400 total), it's 84% cheaper than traditional powered attachments while offering superior versatility.

How It Works
1

Attach Scooter

Position any standard electric scooter next to your wheelchair. The universal QuickClamp mechanism accommodates a wide range of scooter models and widths.

2

Clamp to Chair

Secure the U-Clamp to your wheelchair frame in seconds using the universal mounting points. No tools required. Quick-release design for easy removal.

3

Ride

Control both your scooter and wheelchair independently, or use the integrated control system for coordinated movement. Complete mobility freedom.
