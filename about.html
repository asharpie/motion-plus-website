About Motion+

We're building affordable, modular upgrades that empower people to retrofit their existing wheelchairs and prosthetics instead of replacing them entirely.

Our Story

Motion+ was founded in January 2026 at the University of Alabama by four co-founders united by a singular insight: the assistive technology market has a critical gap. Affordable options underperform. Effective options are unaffordable. We exist in that gap.

Born from the University of Alabama's innovation ecosystem, Motion+ has cultivated an active partnership with UA Adapted Athletics. This collaboration gives us direct access to prototype testing and real-world user feedback—the foundation of meaningful design.

We've earned recognition through national competitions and have secured four provisional patents across our portfolio, validating our commitment to innovative, user-centered assistive technology.

"The market told us a painful truth: people were choosing between affordability and functionality. We decided to reject that choice entirely."

— Motion+ Co-Founders

Our Mission

Retrofit, don't replace.

We believe that innovation in assistive technology shouldn't mean throwing away what works. Our modular upgrades are designed to integrate seamlessly with existing wheelchairs and prosthetics, extending their capabilities at a fraction of the cost of replacement. We're democratizing access to advanced assistive technology, one retrofit at a time.

What Drives Us

We build for the people the market forgot.

01

Nothing ships without user feedback.

Every product in our portfolio was designed alongside the people who use it. Our partnership with UA Adapted Athletics means real athletes test our prototypes in real conditions before anything reaches production.

02

Affordability is not a compromise — it's the mission.

Our products are 10–20× cheaper than the alternatives they replace. That's not a side effect of our engineering — it's the entire point. If the people who need it can't afford it, it doesn't matter how well it works.

03

Upgrade, don't replace.

The industry's default answer is to sell you a whole new system. Ours is to make what you already own work better. Every product we build is modular, retrofittable, and designed to integrate — not dominate — what's already in your life.

04

Prove it, then ship it.

We've won $27,600 in non-dilutive competition funding — not by pitching ideas, but by demonstrating working prototypes. Every claim we make is backed by testing data, user validation, and patent-protected engineering.
